JunkieFund Coverage Expansion
Two products · Two risk profiles · One name you trust

JunkieFund has always existed to absorb risk players knowingly take. That doesn’t mean every risk looks the same.

Today we’re formalizing that reality with two distinct coverage products — one for controlled stacking, and one for going absolutely all-in.

StackShield Coverage
  • Cost: 5 Xanax
  • Coverage: 4 Xanax + 1 Ecstasy
  • Timing: The first Xanax must be taken within 1 hour of buy-in. All remaining Xanax must be taken such that the final Xanax is taken within 24 hours of the first Xanax. Ecstasy must be taken within 32 hours of buy-in.
  • Payout (if OD on Xanax or Ecstasy during window): 5 E-DVDs, 4 Xanax, 1 Ecstasy

StackShield is for deliberate, timed stacking and covers your stack from 0 to 1000.

MainLine Coverage
  • Cost: 1-30 Xanax. Your potential payout is multiplied by the number sent.
  • Window: All drugs must be taken within 2 hours of buy-in
  • Payout (Xanax OD): 35 Xanax
  • Payout (Ecstasy OD): 22 Xanax

MainLine Coverage is classic, single-event protection for players that offers a huge payout. The more you buy in with, the more we multiply your payout if you OD.

Legal Definitions

StackShield Coverage is a voluntary, single-window, item-based coverage arrangement under which a participant tenders a fixed in-game item premium in exchange for eligibility for a defined in-game item payout contingent upon an overdose event occurring within a prescribed multi-drug timing window, subject to published limits and conditions.

MainLine Coverage is a voluntary, single-event, item-based coverage arrangement under which a participant tenders a one-time in-game item premium in exchange for eligibility for a high-value, single-occurrence in-game item payout contingent upon an overdose event occurring within a narrowly defined ingestion window, with no continuing coverage beyond the initial event.
